How Revenue-Based Financing Works

Revenue-based financing is a funding model that aligns with your business's cash flow. Here's a simplified explanation:

In revenue-based financing:

  1. Your business receives a lump sum of capital.
  2. Repayments are tied to a percentage of your monthly revenue.
  3. As your revenue fluctuates, your repayments adjust accordingly.
  4. There are no fixed monthly payments, offering flexibility.
  5. The partnership continues until a predetermined total repayment amount is reached.

This model allows your business to thrive without the pressure of fixed repayments during slower months, making it a flexible and sustainable financing solution.
